Privacy Policy

Home / Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026. This page explains what information Travela collects and how it's used.

1. What we collect

Name, phone number, email and trip preferences submitted through our enquiry and booking forms. For permit-controlled regions, we also collect a copy of government ID as required by local authorities.

2. How it's used

Solely to plan, confirm and support your trip — including sharing necessary details with hotels, drivers and permit offices on your itinerary. We do not sell or rent traveller data to third parties.

3. Marketing communication

If you join our newsletter, you'll receive occasional route guides and seasonal offers. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email we send.

4. Data retention

Booking and ID records are kept for as long as required by Indian travel and tax regulations, after which they are securely deleted.

5. Contact

Questions about your data can be sent to hello@travela.com.
